Resurrect Bot

What is it?

Resurrect your friends. See what they've said in the past[1]

How does it work

We use the latest in AI technology to emulate past users speech pattern and figure out something they might have probably said[1].

Is my data safe?

Probably not, when in doubt, run it yourself

That being said, we store your slack team id, user id, name, and some random words you've said, no full sentences
